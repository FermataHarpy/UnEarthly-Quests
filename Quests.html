Quests


These types of quests are sometimes called scavenger hunts, but some of them are so unique they don't seem to fit in the category of "scavenger hunt". There will be two types of Quests, 21.0 and 1.0, the 21.0 quests are built to be played with alcoholic drinks, but can easily be adjusted to be played without. The 1.0 quests are built to be played by anyone. There is currently only one scavenger hunt available.
